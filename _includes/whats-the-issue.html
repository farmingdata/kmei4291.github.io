What's the Issue?

Over 60% of the population in sub-Saharan Africa work in agriculture. Many are smallholder farmers, producing 80% of the domestic food supply. Their earnings from agriculture are a primary source of household income. For example, in Tanzania, 56% of a smallholder's household income comes from farming.

However, market inefficiency is often an issue. Smallholder farmers face difficulties in selling for better prices and in organising transport logistics.

Given the majority of low- and lower-middle income families in smallholder agriculture, we believe there's a huge opportunity for innovative digital platforms likes ours to streamline market transactions for both sellers and buyers.
