Dr. Jacqui Poon

CEO and Co-founder

Jacqui coordinates business and product development and oversees partnership engagement. She holds a doctorate in plant pathology (2016) from the University of Cambridge and has a background in post-harvest deterioration of cassava (MSc 2012). It was during her cassava project on shelf-life extension when she first developed a passion for improving smallholder agriculture. She believes that empowering small shareholder farmers to access markets can make a positive impact on their communities and their communities and their livelihoods.
